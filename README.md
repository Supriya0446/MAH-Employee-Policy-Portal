# MAH Staff Policy Portal

A modern and responsive Employee Policy Management Portal developed for Maharaja Agrasen Hospital to provide employees with centralized access to workplace policies, HR guidelines, safety regulations, compliance procedures, and organizational information.

The project transforms traditional policy documentation into an interactive digital platform, making important employee information easier to search, understand, and access.

---

# Project Overview

The MAH Staff Policy Portal is a front-end web application designed to organize and present hospital employee policies in a structured and user-friendly format.

The portal provides employees with a single platform to explore important organizational guidelines related to:

- Employee rights and workplace entitlements
- Professional responsibilities and conduct
- HR operations and grievance handling
- Resignation and exit procedures
- Workplace safety and emergency preparedness
- Information security and confidentiality
- Employee records management
- Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) procedures
- Governance and compliance standards

The application focuses on improving policy accessibility, reducing information gaps, and providing a professional digital reference system for hospital staff.

---

# Key Features

## Interactive Policy Management

- Accordion-based policy sections for organized content presentation
- Expand and collapse functionality for better readability
- Smooth navigation to specific policy sections
- Structured policy cards with categorized information

## Smart Policy Search

- Real-time search functionality
- Searches across policy titles and detailed content
- Automatically filters relevant policy information
- Displays no-result handling for unavailable searches

## Category-Based Filtering

Users can quickly filter policies based on different categories:

- All Policies
- Employee Policies
- HR Operations
- Safety
- Information Management
- Records Management
- Governance
- Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)

This allows employees to quickly locate information related to their requirements.

# Application Workflow

1. User opens the Employee Policy Portal.
2. The homepage displays hospital information, vision, mission, and core values.
3. Employees can:
   - Browse available policies
   - Search required information
   - Filter policies by category
   - Open detailed policy sections
   - Navigate directly through the menu
   - Submit feedback

The interface provides a simple and efficient way to access important workplace information.
